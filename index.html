About Us

Welcome to Nepali Style by Palpali, where we bring you the authentic flavors of Palpa, a historic region in western Nepal. Known for its rich culinary traditions, Palpa offers unique recipes passed down through generations. Our pickles and jams are handcrafted using traditional methods and fresh ingredients sourced from local farms, capturing the essence of Palpa's heritage in every jar.

We are launching our products during Dashain, Nepal's biggest festival. Dashain signifies the victory of good over evil and is celebrated with family gatherings, feasts, and worship. We aim to share the essence of our culture through food with the people of New Zealand.
